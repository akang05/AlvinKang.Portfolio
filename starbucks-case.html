Starbucks App: Heuristic Optimization

UX Research | Feb - Mar 2026

Heuristic Evaluation

Using Nielsen’s 10 Heuristics, I identified significant friction in the "Customization" phase of mobile ordering. Users struggled with "Visibility of System Status" when items were out of stock.

Affinity Diagramming

I synthesized data from 5 usability tests to create an affinity diagram, grouping pain points into: Navigation, Feedback, and Error Prevention.

Results

The redesigned user flow optimized the "checkout" journey, reducing the time from selection to payment by 15 seconds. My research focused on "Recognition rather than Recall" for frequent orders.
