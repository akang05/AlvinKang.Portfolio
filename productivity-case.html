Redesigning Productivity for the Modern Student

UX Research & Design | Jan - Feb 2026

The Problem

Students face "choice paralysis" and cognitive overload when using complex task managers. Our team (with Aslan, Effie, and Ellie) identified that current apps focus too much on features and not enough on the mental state of the user.

User Research

We conducted 1:1 user interviews and developed empathy maps to understand the emotional journey of a student during finals week.

  • Key Insight: Users don't want more folders; they want the app to tell them what is due now.

The Solution

A "Lite" interface focusing on "The One Thing"—the single most important task for the next 2 hours. We used Figma to prototype a high-fidelity solution that reduced navigation clicks by 40%.
